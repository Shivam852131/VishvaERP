Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026

1. Data Collection

Vishva ERP collects institutional and personal data necessary for academic operations, including but not limited to names, emails, roll numbers, attendance records, and financial transactions.

2. Native App & Push Notifications

Our Android and iOS applications collect device tokens to provide real-time campus alerts. We do not track user location in the background. Location data is only used with explicit consent for smart classroom attendance.

3. Data Security

We employ enterprise-grade encryption for all data at rest and in transit. Institutional data is isolated using multi-tenant architecture.

# Vishva ERP Privacy Policy (Template)

**Last updated:** June 2026

Vishva ERP ("we", "our", "the platform") provides campus management software for educational institutions.

## Information We Collect

- **Account data**: Name, email, phone, role, institution affiliation
- **Academic data**: Attendance, grades, assignments, timetable, fees
- **Usage data**: Login timestamps, audit logs, device tokens for notifications
- **Optional location**: Classroom geofence signals for consent-based smart attendance only

## How We Use Information

- Deliver ERP features to authorized users within their institution
- Send academic notices, fee reminders, and exam alerts via in-app and push notifications
- Improve platform security and operational analytics

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only:

- Within the user's institution based on role permissions
- With infrastructure providers (hosting, email, push notification services) under contract

## Data Retention

Institutions control retention through their admin policies. Deactivated accounts lose portal access; data may be retained per institutional compliance requirements.

## Security

- HTTPS encryption in transit
- Role-based access control
- JWT authentication with rate limiting
- Optional two-factor authentication (institution setting)

## Your Rights

Users may request profile updates through their institution admin or platform support. Account deactivation requests should be directed to the institution's College Admin or Vishva ERP support.

## Children's Privacy

The platform is intended for use by educational institutions. Institutions are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent for student data.

## Contact

- Support: support@vishvaerp.com
- Privacy: privacy@vishvaerp.com
